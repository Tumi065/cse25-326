Cleaning & Care

Cleaning your watch keeps sweat and dirt from damaging the metal and the seals that keep water out. It also prevents scratches and kills bacteria that can cause skin rashes or bad Odors caused by sweat buildup. This habit allows you to keep your watch looking new and working perfectly for a much longer time.

Repairs & Maintenance

Getting your watch serviced keeps the internal parts lubricated so they don't grind down and break down after extended periods of time. It also replaces the seals to keep water and rust out of the delicate internal movement. This simple care keeps your watch accurate and helps you avoid expensive repairs in the future.

Customization Options

Customizing your watch lets you change out parts like the dial (face) or strap to create a look that is unique to you. It turns a basic accessory into a personal piece of art that perfectly matches your style and personality. This change makes your watch feel more special and meaningful every time you wear it.
